## Information Collection and Use

### Data you enter in the app

JuleKonkurrencer is designed as a simple tool to keep track of Christmas calendar competitions. In the app you can add information such as:

* Name/title of a competition
* Type (website, app, physical, email, etc.)
* Links to external websites
* Notes you write yourself

This information is stored **locally on your device** and is only used to show and manage your list of competitions. We do **not** upload this user-entered content to our own servers.

To improve the app and understand how it is used, we may use analytics services (for example Firebase Analytics or similar). These services can collect information such as:

This data is generally collected in an aggregated or pseudonymous form and is used only for:

* Monitoring stability and performance
* Understanding which features are used

## Cookies

JuleKonkurrencer itself does **not** use cookies.

However, when you open external websites from the app (for example competition pages in your browser), those websites may use cookies to remember your login, form data or preferences. These cookies are managed by the websites themselves, not by JuleKonkurrencer.

You can control cookies via your browser settings and the cookie banners on those websites.
